Purchase Order Bank

When the user clicks on the “three dots” in front of the PO Number, they can see a Purchase Order Bank icon. After clicking on the icon, they can access it.

After filling in the details and clicking on the “Save” button, the user can add a Purchase Order Bank.

When the Payment Method changes, the information to be filled in the Purchase Order bank also changes accordingly.
